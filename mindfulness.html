Calm Your Mind

Take a Breather

The National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH) highlights the notable benefits of mindfulness and relaxation for your mental health. Mindfulness practices like meditation, Diaphragmatic Breathing, visualization, assisting individuals, focusing on the present moment, and managing overwhelming thoughts or emotions. The NIMH notes that these techniques can reduce stress and enhance your emotional regulation, assisting you in building resilience towards life's challenges. For teenagers, incorporating mindfulness into daily activites & routines can improve their mood and reduce anxiety.

Try This

Even 5 minutes of deep breathing daily can measurably reduce stress hormones and improve focus.
