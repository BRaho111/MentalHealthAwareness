Superman - Movie Review

Overview

Title: Superman
Director: James Gunn
Release Year: 2025th Edition
Rating: 8.3/10*

Plot Summary

Superman is born Kal-El on the alien planet Krypton. Krypton's impending destruction causes his parents to send Superman to Earth. Superman has a companion Lois Lane and encounters an enemy Lex Luthor.

Review

I honestly don't see how this movie could impress anyone older than 10. Sure, it's colorful, lighthearted, and has a few crowd-pleasing moments - but underneath the cape and charm, it feels more like a Saturday morning cartoon than the cinematic relaunch of an iconic hero. For a character as emotionally rich and symbolically heavy as Superman, this version felt oddly flat, even juvenile.

Highlights

Visual Effects: Amazing Quality, the movie is extremely appealing to the eyes.
Acting: Stellar performances with the main actor Henry Cavill displaying his talents.
Music: The music was lackluster and disappointing.

Critics Notes

*The film felt rushed, overloaded plot, and goofiness.
Some scenes may not be suitable for children under 12.
